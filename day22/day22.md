# Day 22 — Startup Validation with Claude

## What I Did
Used Claude to run a full startup validation analysis on my EdTech platform idea as part of the #60DayClaudeChallenge.

## Key Assumption Tested
> "Students struggle to find structured, affordable, and personalized learning paths online."

**Result: ✅ VALIDATED — The problem is real and painful.**

## What Claude Analyzed
- Founder-Market Fit Assessment
- TAM, SAM & SOM Market Sizing
- Competitor & Market Gap Analysis
- Customer Persona & Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)
- Risk Assessment
- Go / No-Go Recommendation
- 30-Day Action Plan

## Key Learnings
- AI can compress weeks of market research into a single focused conversation
- Having honest answers during discovery questions leads to sharper insights
- Startup validation isn't just about confirming ideas — it's about finding blind spots early
