# Day 37: Task Compass - Management Simulation 🧭

Built an interactive management simulation application using Claude to explore organizational workflows, task delegation, and cross-functional collaboration through gameplay.

### What This App Does:
* **Ownership Mapping:** Evaluates who should own specific workplace tasks and why.
* **Workflow & Routing:** Simulates how work moves across different departments in real-time organizational structures.
* **Collaboration Challenges:** Creates scenarios demonstrating why complex problems require multiple specialized teams.
* **Organizational Thinking Dashboard:** Tracks user decisions, providing an analytical summary of management styles across different workplace types.

### Files Included:
* `task_compass_simulation.html` - The standalone interactive application.
* `day37.md` - Key takeaways, analysis, and screenshots of the Organizational Thinking Dashboard.
