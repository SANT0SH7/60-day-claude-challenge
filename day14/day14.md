# Day 14 — Job Red Flag Detector with Claude 🚨

## What I Built
Used Claude as a career intelligence tool to analyze job descriptions and company information for hidden risks before applying.

## What You'll Learn
- Risk Detection: Identify hidden red flags inside job descriptions
- Company Analysis: Evaluate company stability and reputation signals
- Remote Validation: Detect misleading remote-work claims
- Interview Strategy: Generate smart questions to validate concerns

## How It Works
1. Set Claude effort level to Low
2. Gather a Job Description (JD) — uploaded as PDF or pasted directly
3. Paste company information alongside it
4. Use the Job Red Flag Detector prompt
5. Claude generates a structured Risk Analysis Report including:
   - Overall Risk Score
   - Red Flags & Positive Signals
   - Risk Breakdown by category
   - Final Verdict
   - Tailored Interview Questions

## Key Insight
A job application is a two-way evaluation. Claude helps candidates evaluate opportunities the same way companies evaluate candidates.
