Prior Authorization Story Simulator
An interactive story-based educational application that teaches the Prior Authorization process through character-driven narrative and branching dialogue. Follow Rahul's patient journey and Priya's expert guidance through 8 interactive chapters where your choices shape the outcome.
Features

Interactive Storytelling: Narrative-driven Prior Authorization education with relatable characters
Branching Dialogue: Conversational decision trees where every choice impacts the story
Character Narratives:

Rahul: Patient experiencing authorization challenges
Priya: Healthcare expert providing real-time guidance and explanations


8 Story Chapters: Complete healthcare journey from diagnosis → treatment approval
Dynamic Progress Tracking: Visual progress bar following story progression
Choice-Based Learning: Explore different approval paths and understand consequences
Real-World Scenarios: Denial handling, appeals, and resolution workflows

What You'll Learn

Prior Authorization process through character experience
Patient journey pain points and decision-making
Provider communication strategies
Appeal processes and outcome scenarios
Healthcare operations from a human perspective
