# Day 25 — AI Shark Tank Simulator 🦈

**#60DayClaudeChallenge | Day 25**

## Overview
Built a complete AI-powered Shark Tank simulation app using Claude. Enter a startup idea and face a panel of AI investors who evaluate your pitch, ask challenging questions, and deliver an investment decision.

## What's Inside
- 🤖 AI investor personas with unique evaluation styles
- 📊 Startup scorecard (market, execution, revenue potential)
- 💬 Interactive Q&A with AI judges
- 💰 Valuation & funding recommendation engine
- 🏆 Leaderboard functionality
- 📄 Downloadable pitch report

## Key Learnings
- How investors evaluate startup opportunities
- Communicating startup value effectively under pressure
- Building complete business-focused web applications with AI
