# Day 12 — Job Search & Personal Branding Toolkit

## What I built today
Used Claude to generate a complete career toolkit tailored to a target role and job description.

## Tools used
- Claude (claude.ai) — Sonnet model, effort level: Low

## What the toolkit covers
- **ATS optimization** — Resume restructured with recruiter-friendly keywords and formatting
- **Recruiter outreach** — Cold message templates with higher response-rate framing
- **Personal branding** — Positioning statement aligned to target role
- **Skill gap analysis** — Identified missing skills and growth areas based on the JD

## Key learning
Modern hiring evaluates far more than your resume. Claude can map your actual experience to what a specific JD is looking for — and surface gaps you'd otherwise miss. The prompt + real JD combo is the unlock.

## Workflow
1. Uploaded resume
2. Pasted a target JD
3. Ran the Job Search & Personal Branding prompt
4. Reviewed ATS recommendations and skill gap output
5. Ran a second improvement prompt for refinement
6. Saved outputs and took screenshots
