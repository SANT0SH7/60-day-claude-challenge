# Day 29: Build Operation Lifeline - Supply Chain Crisis Lab

## Project Overview
This repository contains the deliverables for Day 29 of the #60DayClaudeChallenge. In this lab, an interactive React-based HTML enterprise application simulator was generated using Claude to evaluate executive decision-making under operational duress. The simulation challenges leaders to successfully navigate supplier relationships, customer satisfaction, cost structures, and business continuity during a severe supply chain disruption.

## Key Learnings & Competencies
* **Supply Chain Strategy:** Explored organizational risk-mitigation frameworks and strategic response protocols during live disruptions.
* **Business Decision Making:** Practiced balancing complex enterprise trade-offs across cost structures, inventory controls, and customer-facing KPIs.
* **AI in Operations:** Investigated the strategic implementation of artificial intelligence for predictive forecasting, procurement, and risk management.
* **Interactive Web Apps:** Utilized LLM capabilities to build an enterprise-level React simulator bundled into a portable, standalone HTML file.
