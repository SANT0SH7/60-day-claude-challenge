# Stock Fundamental Research Skill

A reusable Claude Custom Skill for analyzing and comparing Indian stocks with comprehensive fundamental research.

## What This Skill Does
- Analyzes financial metrics, valuations, and ownership structures
- Generates structured fundamental research reports
- Compares multiple stocks side-by-side
- Identifies key risk factors and investment insights
- Provides actionable stock analysis frameworks

## Tech Stack
- Claude Custom Skills (API)
- Stock analysis prompts & frameworks
- Indian stock market data (TCS, Infosys, Reliance, HDFC Bank, Tata Motors)

## Key Features
✅ Reusable across unlimited conversations  
✅ Structured stock comparison analysis  
✅ Ownership trends & risk factor analysis  
✅ No need to re-enter complex prompts  

## Learning Outcomes
- How to create permanent Claude capabilities
- Stock fundamental research framework
- Productivity through workflow automation
