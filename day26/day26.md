Prior Authorization Workflow Simulator
An interactive HTML-based simulator that models the healthcare Prior Authorization process—the critical workflow between patients, healthcare providers, and insurers that determines treatment approval.
Features

Workflow Simulation: Real-world Prior Authorization stages with realistic decision trees
Gamified Learning: Drag-and-drop document assembly and submission mechanics
Interactive Scenarios: Multiple patient profiles with different authorization outcomes (approval, denial, appeal)
Progress Tracking: Visual journey tracker showing elapsed days and workflow stage
Educational Explainers: Inline guidance on healthcare operations and approval logic

What You'll Learn

How Prior Authorization impacts patient care flow
Document requirements for authorization requests
Provider and insurer decision-making criteria
Appeal and denial handling in healthcare operations
