Day 23: EV Distribution Startup - Validation Report & MVP Blueprint

Summary:
Completed comprehensive market validation for EV sales & distribution platform.
Analyzed dealer pain points, customer acquisition barriers, and feature priorities for MVP.

Key Customer Insights:
- First-time EV buyers prioritize financing options & service guarantees over range/specs
- Dealers lack centralized platform for inventory, leads, test drives, and follow-up workflows
- Corporate fleet buyers want transparent ROI & TCO comparison tools
- Tier-2 cities (Pune, Jaipur, Ahmedabad) show higher adoption intent than metro saturation
- Financing bottleneck: 60% of interested buyers drop off due to loan approval complexity

MVP Validation:
- Ideal Customer Profile: Mid-sized EV dealers (50-200 units/year) in Tier-2 cities
- Buyer Persona: Dealership operations managers seeking to reduce manual CRM work
- Key Pain Points: Lead tracking, scheduling, financing orchestration, customer follow-up
- Feature Prioritization: CRM + financing integration >> inventory management >> analytics

MVP Roadmap (4 months):
Phase 1: Dealer CRM + test drive scheduling
Phase 2: Financing partner integration (NBFC workflows)
Phase 3: Customer ROI calculator + post-purchase service coordination

Go-to-Market:
- Target: 2-3 pilot dealers in Tier-2 cities
- Value prop: 40% reduction in lead-to-close time + transparent financing
- Revenue: SaaS subscription (₹15K-30K/month per dealer)

Next Steps:
- Dealer interviews & feedback loops
- Financing partner outreach
- MVP development sprint
